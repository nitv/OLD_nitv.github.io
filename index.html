About Me

I am a doctoral student at the School of Information at The University of Texas at Austin. I study trust in visual representations such as photographs, videos, and information visualizations. My doctoral research is supervised by Dr. Kenneth R. Fleischmann, Dr. Jacek Gwizdka, Dr. James Howison, and Dr. Sharon Strover.
